Best Bars & Craft Drinks in Barranquilla

Mike Chartrand
April 17, 2026 — Updated April 23, 2026 · 3 min read

Barranquilla’s bar scene has matured considerably in recent years, moving well beyond the rum-and-beer standard to offer genuinely creative cocktail programmes, craft brewing, and sophisticated drink destinations. The northern districts are the main hunting ground, with options ranging from rooftop bars with city-wide views to intimate craft beer taprooms. Here’s where to drink well.

1. Lobo Cocktail Bar

Signature Cocktails Outdoor Terrace $$

Riomar  ·  Cra. 53 #85-61

The leading cocktail bar in the city. Lobo’s menu is compact but carefully curated – the bartenders know each drink intimately and make them with precision. The outdoor terrace is the best seat in the house on warm evenings (which is most evenings in Barranquilla). The atmosphere is chic without being cold, and the food programme – particularly the sushi – makes it a destination in its own right rather than just a pre-club stop.

Signature drink: The Boka Legend. Order it first, decide what comes next from there.

2. Fierabras Punto de Fábrica

Craft Beer On-Site Brewery $

Barranquilla  ·  Cra. 53 #46-192

The best craft brewery in Barranquilla. Three signature beers brewed on-site, the most celebrated of which is the Dulcinea – a brown ale with coffee and dark chocolate notes that tastes unlike anything else available in the city. The atmosphere is relaxed and musical, the prices are extremely reasonable, and it remains genuinely local in spirit despite its reputation. A must for anyone who takes their beer seriously.

Must-try: Dulcinea brown ale. Pair with nachos for a full evening.

3. Kava Rooftop & Gastro-Bar

Rooftop Bar City Views $$$

Barranquilla Norte

For drinking with altitude and perspective. Kava’s rooftop perch offers some of the best views of Barranquilla at night, and the cocktail and food programme is worthy of the setting. It’s ideal for sunset drinks, a post-dinner cocktail with a group, or any occasion where you want the evening to feel elevated in every sense. The crowd skews upscale, the service is polished, and the vibe tends toward the sophisticated end of the spectrum.

Best time to go: Arrive 30 minutes before sunset – the view during the golden hour is exceptional.

4. Bogotá Beer Company

Craft Beer Pub Food $$

Barranquilla Norte  ·  Cra. 53 #82-86 Local 12

Colombia’s best-known craft beer brand, represented in Barranquilla with a venue that delivers consistently on the promise. Some of the best craft beer available in the country, paired with above-average pub food – the burgers with fries are particularly well regarded. A reliable, comfortable choice for a group that wants quality beer in a familiar, convivial setting without hunting down a hidden taproom.

Best for: Groups who want quality craft beer in an unpretentious, sociable space.

5. Mi Talismán Bar

Local Bar Authentic Culture $

Barranquilla

A vibrant, authentically Barranquillero bar that offers a genuine glimpse into local nightlife culture. Mi Talismán isn’t trying to be anything other than what it is – a great local bar with expertly made drinks, infectious energy, and a crowd that’s happy to be exactly where they are. It’s beloved by travellers who want to experience the real character of the city rather than an internationally standardised bar experience. Expect excellent local beverages, genuine hospitality, and the kind of night that turns into a good story.

Best for: Travellers who want to drink like a local; a genuinely cultural bar experience unique to Barranquilla.

What to drink: Beyond cocktails and craft beer, Barranquilla’s bar culture is built on ron (rum). Colombia’s Ron Medellín and the Caribbean-coast favourite Ron de la Caña are both excellent. Ask any bartender for their preferred preparation and you’ll likely get an education – and a very good drink.
