Best Gyms in Barranquilla

Paulette Romero
August 30, 2023 — Updated April 23, 2026 · 4 min read

Barranquilla is a hub of fitness enthusiasts, wellness seekers, and home to some of the best gyms. With a multitude of gyms catering to various workout preferences, residents and visitors alike have ample options to stay fit and active. In this article, we delve into three prominent gyms in Barranquilla – Spinning Center, Smartfit, and Bodytech – and explore the unique offerings that make them stand out in the city’s fitness landscape.

1. Spinning Center

Nestled on the fourth floor of Buenavista 2, Spinning Center isn’t just for cycling aficionados seeking an intense cardiovascular workout. The gym offers a dynamic and high-energy atmosphere that motivates members to push their limits. It’s a full gym with all the regular gym equipment and free weights you would find at any gym. The instructors at Spinning Center are seasoned professionals. The club’s cutting-edge equipment, coupled with an engaging playlist, ensures that members are not only burning calories but also having a blast while doing so. At the time of publishing a 10-month gym plan runs about $600,000 COP. If you’re looking for a personal trainer, 12 semi-personalized classes will set you back $375,000 COP. Group classes are included in the price of the membership. Additionally, Spinning Center operates a sports club next to the gym where members and guests can enjoy bowling and billiards. 2. Smartfit

For fitness enthusiasts who seek quality workouts without straining their wallets, Smartfit stands as a game-changer. This gym chain has established its presence across Barranquilla, offering an affordable yet comprehensive fitness experience. Smartfit boasts an array of modern workout equipment and spacious facilities. The gym’s approach focuses on providing a no-frills, results-driven environment where members can customize their fitness journey. With access to expert trainers and a variety of group classes, Smartfit empowers individuals of all fitness levels to reach their health goals without breaking the bank. The lowest monthly fee will set you back around $80,000 COP per month. A personal trainer can run you about $350,000 to $400,000 COP per month. Smartfit has eight gyms in Barranquilla.

3. Bodytech

When it comes to luxury and a holistic approach to fitness, Bodytech takes center stage in Barranquilla. This upscale gym offers an integrated wellness experience that extends beyond the typical workout routine. The facility boasts top-of-the-line fitness equipment, immaculate interiors, and a range of amenities that cater to the discerning fitness enthusiast. What sets Bodytech apart is its emphasis on overall well-being, offering services such as personal training, nutritional guidance, and relaxation options like spa treatments and yoga classes. By blending fitness with self-care, Bodytech encourages its members to adopt a balanced and sustainable approach to health. Bodytech is by far the most expensive of the three chain gyms. A one-year membership will cost you about $1,345,000 COP. A personal trainer will set you back about $350,000 to $400,000 COP per month.

4. Action Black

Action Black opened in Barranquilla in 2024. Their tagline is “We’re not a f**king gym.” According to them, they have the most revolutionary training model in the world. They currently have one location in Barranquilla at Altos del Prado Mall. A second location is opening at Viva Barranquilla soon. At publishing, their annual membership fee was $2,400,000 COP.

5. The Bronx Gym

The Bronx Gym has two locations in Barranquilla. Their Sede Gold (Gold Location) is located on Carrera 51B with Calle 80 in Alto Prado. Their Sede Silver is located in Villa Campestre on Calle 135 at Brazuca Mall. At the Sede Silver, their daily rate is $70,000 COP. Their monthly rate is $110,000 COP. Their annual rate is $1,056,000 COP.

Conclusion

Barranquilla’s fitness scene is as diverse as its culture, and the city’s gyms reflect this diversity in their offerings. From the adrenaline-pumping sessions at Spinning Center to the budget-friendly workouts at Smartfit and the luxury wellness experience at Bodytech, each gym caters to a specific set of preferences. Whether you’re an avid cyclist, a cost-conscious individual, or someone who values comprehensive wellness, Barranquilla’s gyms have something unique to offer. So, whether you’re a resident looking to revamp your fitness routine or a visitor eager to stay active during your stay, exploring these gyms can open the door to a healthier and happier you.
