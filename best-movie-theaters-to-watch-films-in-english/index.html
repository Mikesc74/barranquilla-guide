Best Movie Theaters to watch films in English

Paulette Romero
September 3, 2023 — Updated April 23, 2026 · 3 min read

For those seeking a cinematic experience with English movies, the city boasts several top-notch theaters that cater to moviegoers’ preferences. Barranquilla offers a diverse range of entertainment options to residents and visitors. Among the finest choices are Cinepolis in Mallplaza, Cine Colombia in Buenavista, Royal Films in Viva, Royal Films in Villa Country, and Cine Colombia in Multiplex Parque Alegra. These theaters promise not only the latest blockbusters but also a truly immersive and captivating movie experience.

Pro Tip: To find the films in English look for the option that says SUB which means subtitled.

1. Cinépolis VIP in Mallplaza

With its state-of-the-art facilities and commitment to delivering the ultimate movie-watching experience, Cinepolis VIP in Mallplaza stands as one of Barranquilla’s premier theaters. This theater utilizes cutting-edge audiovisual technology to bring the magic of Hollywood to life. Patrons can enjoy the comfort of plush leather seating while they watch a wide selection of English-language movies. The theater shows films in English with Spanish subtitles, enhancing the viewing experience. The experience comes complete with waiters that take your order at your seat. Cocktails and beer are also available.

2. Cine Colombia in Buenavista

Situated in the bustling Buenavista shopping complex, Cine Colombia brings the allure of English-language cinema to Barranquilla. The theater prides itself on its diverse array of movie choices from international studios. With top-tier projection and sound systems, every detail of the film is presented with utmost clarity, enabling audiences to be fully immersed in the captivating world of cinema. It also boasts a 4D Dinamix theater.

3. Royal Films in Viva

Royal Films at Viva is synonymous with entertainment, making it an excellent choice for English movie enthusiasts. The theater’s commitment to delivering quality shows is evident through its comfortable seating arrangements and exceptional visual and audio technology. Whether it’s an action-packed blockbuster or a heartwarming drama, Royal Films ensures that every movie shines on the big screen. Viva has a 4DX theater that allows films to be augmented with various practical effects, including motion-seats, wind, strobe-lights, simulated-snow, and scents.

4. Cine Colombia in Multiplex Parque Alegra

As part of the Cine Colombia chain, the Multiplex Parque Alegra branch maintains the brand’s reputation for excellence. This theater is a prime destination for English movie enthusiasts, providing an extensive lineup of films that cater to various preferences. With its modern facilities, the theater is the newest addition to Barranquilla. It has a Megasala and 3D theater room. This mall mostly services the residents of southern Barranquilla, but shows films in English on weekends.

Conclusion

Barranquilla’s cinematic landscape is adorned with an array of outstanding theaters that offer the best in English-language movie entertainment. Whether it’s the cutting-edge technology at Cinepolis in Mallplaza, the diverse film choices at Cine Colombia in Buenavista, or the innovative movie experiences at Cine Colombia in Multiplex Parque Alegra, these theaters collectively provide a gateway to the captivating world of English-language cinema. So, if you’re a movie enthusiast in Barranquilla, these theaters are sure to provide you with an unforgettable cinematic journey.
