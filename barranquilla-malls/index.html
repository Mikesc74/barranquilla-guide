Exploring Barranquilla’s Vibrant Malls

Paulette Romero
September 9, 2023 — Updated April 23, 2026 · 3 min read

Barranquilla is not only known for its rich cultural heritage and lively festivals but also for its thriving malls. With an array of malls catering to diverse tastes and preferences, this city offers an unparalleled shopping experience for locals and tourists alike. In this article, we will take an active look at some of Barranquilla’s most popular malls, each with its unique charm and attractions.

Buenavista 1 and 2

Buenavista Mall is a retail haven that stands as a symbol of modernity and convenience in Barranquilla. Located in the heart of the city, it offers a wide range of national and international brands, making it a favorite among fashion enthusiasts. From clothing boutiques to electronics stores, Buenavista has it all. In addition to shopping, visitors can indulge in delectable culinary delights at its numerous restaurants or catch a movie at the cinema. The Buenavista shopping center is made up of three buildings. The first two are Buenavista 1 and 2. The third is Mallaplaza Buenavista.

Supermarkets: Éxito and Jumbo

Specialty Stores: Rolex, Montblanc, Levi’s, Samsung, Speedo, Tommy Hilfiger, Lacoste, MAC Cosmetics, Pandora, Zara

Gym: Spinning Center

Theater: Cine Colombia

Mallplaza (Buenavista 3)

Mallplaza is another shopping gem that beckons visitors with its expansive retail spaces and a diverse range of stores. This mall boasts a mix of local and international brands, ensuring that shoppers can find everything they need under one roof. Moreover, Mallplaza is a family-friendly destination, complete with an entertainment zone for kids and a variety of dining options for all tastes. Mallplaza is located next to Buenavista 1 and 2 and across the street from Plaza del Parque.

Supermarket: Carulla

Specialty Stores: Homecenter, Falabella, Bath & Body Works, Invicta, Malva, Nike, Under Armour, Victoria’s Secret, Zara Home

Gym: Smartfit

Theater: Cinépolis VIP

Viva

Viva Barranquilla is a lifestyle destination that combines shopping with entertainment. This mall is known for its vibrant atmosphere and hosts events and exhibitions regularly. Shoppers can explore an eclectic collection of stores, from fashion boutiques to tech outlets. What sets Viva apart is its outdoor spaces, perfect for enjoying the city’s tropical climate while sipping on a coffee or enjoying a meal.

Supermarket: Éxito

Specialty Stores: Adidas, Bath & Body Works, Decathlon, H&M, Mercedes Campuzano

Gym: Bodytech

Theater: Royal Films

Villa Country

Villa Country Mall is synonymous with luxury and sophistication. It caters to those with discerning tastes, offering a curated selection of high-end fashion, jewelry, and lifestyle brands. If you’re looking for exclusive shopping and a taste of elegance, Villa Country is the place to be. The mall also features gourmet dining options for those seeking a culinary adventure.

Supermarket: Carulla

Le Meridiem Golf

Le Meridiem Golf, partially under construction, mostly houses restaurants with a few clothing stores. There is an Olimpica supermarket on the bottom floor and a new Padel tennis court on the top floor. With numerous dining options, the mall can get quite busy on weekends. This is one of the newest malls in Barranquilla and is showing lots of potential and interest from local residents.

Parque Alegra

Parque Alegra, located on Calle 30 in the south of Barranquilla, offers a refreshing change of pace from the bustling city center. This mall opened in 2022 and is the larges mall in the south of the city. Here, visitors can discover a mix of retail shops, along with a range of dining options serving both local and international cuisine. A gym and movie theater are also found here.

Supermarket: SAO/Olimpica

Gym: Smartfit

Theater: Cine Colombia

Conclusion

Malls in Barranquilla offer a diverse and dynamic shopping experience for residents and visitors. Whether you’re looking for the latest fashion trends, high-end luxury items, family-friendly entertainment, or a unique shopping niche like golf, these malls have something for everyone. So, the next time you find yourself in Barranquilla, be sure to explore these fantastic retail destinations and immerse yourself in the city’s vibrant shopping culture.
