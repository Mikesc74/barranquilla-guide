Caribbean Night Break Barranquilla: Nightlife Tour Review (2026)

Mike Chartrand
April 9, 2026 — Updated April 23, 2026 · 2 min read

The Caribbean Night Break is one of Barranquilla’s most distinctive experiences – a guided evening motorcycle and vehicle tour that takes you through the city’s illuminated landmarks, along the coast to Puerto Colombia, and into the bars and nightlife spots where locals actually go. It holds a 5.0-star rating and is unlike anything else on offer in the city.

What the Tour Covers

  • Barranquilla by night – The Gran Malecón, the historic El Prado neighbourhood, and the city’s best illuminated landmarks look completely different after dark
  • Puerto Colombia – The historic beach town 20 minutes from the city, with its famous pier lit up at night against the Caribbean
  • Local bars and nightlife stops – The guide takes you to spots the tourist circuit doesn’t know about
  • Motorcycle tour element – a uniquely costeño way to experience the city

Who This Is For

This tour suits visitors who want more than the standard daytime city tour – people who want to experience Barranquilla the way it actually lives, which is very much after dark. It’s also a safe and guided way to explore the nightlife for visitors who want local knowledge rather than figuring it out alone.

The 5.0 rating reflects how well it delivers on its promise: an authentic, local evening experience that most visitors would never find independently.

Practical Details

  • Duration: 4 to 5 hours
  • Price: From ~$68 USD (COP 284,007)
  • Departure: Evening – typically 7pm or 8pm start
  • Cancellation: Free cancellation
  • What to wear: Smart casual – you’ll be going into bars. Not flip-flops.
  • Safety: Fully guided throughout. Helmets provided for motorcycle element.

Before the Tour: Where to Stay

El Prado is the right base – both for this tour and for your broader stay. Hotel El Prado and Dann Carlton are both excellent options with convenient evening access to the nightlife zone.
