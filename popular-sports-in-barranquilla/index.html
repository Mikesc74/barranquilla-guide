Popular Sports for Enthusiasts in Barranquilla

Paulette Romero
August 16, 2023 — Updated April 23, 2026 · 5 min read

Barranquilla, a vibrant city nestled on the Caribbean coast of Colombia, is not only renowned for its rich cultural heritage and pulsating festivals but also for the myriad sports activities it offers. From the sea breeze-kissed shores to the well-maintained parks, Barranquilla provides ample opportunities for sports enthusiasts to indulge in their passion and lead an active lifestyle. Let’s delve into the exciting array of sports that locals and visitors alike can enjoy in Barranquilla.

1. Football (Soccer)

Football, otherwise known as soccer to North Americans, is a passion that runs deep in Barranquilla’s veins. With a plethora of well-maintained fields and community parks, finding a spot to kick the ball around or join a local game is a breeze. Joining a soccer match in Barranquilla is not just a physical activity; it’s an immersion into the city’s vibrant culture and unity. According to the history books, football first arrived in Colombia by some Englishmen who landed in Barranquilla. The first football league was started in Atlántico at the beginning of the 20th century.

2. Tennis and Racquetball

After football, tennis is one of the most popular sports in Barranquilla. Tennis aficionados will be pleased to discover that Barranquilla offers courts for both casual play and competitive matches. Enjoy a friendly match with friends or partake in local tournaments to test your skills against fellow players. The two biggest tennis courts are located at Parque Distrital de Raquetas and Bosques del Norte. Be sure to join the Barranquilla Tennis Lovers group on Whatsapp to find a buddy to play with. If racquetball is more your style, there are four courts at Parque Distrital de Raquetas. Remember to reserve the court in advance using the Alcaldía’s online reservation system. Want some racquetball lessons? Reach out to racquetball trainer, Edwin, at Parque de Raquetas.

3. Golf

FORE! Lagos de Caujaral boasts the third-best golf course in Colombia for golf aficionados. This 18-hole golf course welcomes both amateurs and professionals. Caujaral offers a special rate for visiting foreigners which at the time of publishing was $300,000 COP. Personalized classes with a golf instructor are also available. Reach out to golf instructor, Oscar, for rates and times. Classes are given in Spanish, although classes in English may be available with a different instructor.

4. Water Sports

Given its coastal location, Atlántico provides a paradise for water sports enthusiasts. Kitesurfing, surfing, and paddleboarding are just a few of the activities that allow you to harness the power of the sea while enjoying the sun and water. Puerto Mocho, Puerto Colombia and, Santa Veronica are some of the best spots to practice these sports. Be sure to hire a professional to train you in these sports. The Gobernación del Atlántico even has a PDF guide available for download detailing the best places to practice various water sports and where to find a coach or instructor.

5. Martial Arts

For those seeking a more holistic approach to fitness, Barranquilla offers a range of martial arts options. Engage your mind and body through disciplines like taekwondo and muay thai. The American Taekwondo Center in Villa Campestre offers classes to both kids and adults. Additionally, several training centers in Barranquilla have embraced Muay Thai, offering training programs for individuals of all skill levels. For more information, get in touch with the Muay Thai League or BarranKilla Muay Thai.

6. Running and Jogging

For those who prefer to break a sweat on their own terms, Barranquilla’s riverfront boulevard, the Gran Malecón del Rio, offers excellent opportunities for running and jogging. The Gran Malecón a picturesque boardwalk that runs along the Magdalena River, is a popular spot for runners to soak in the city’s natural beauty while staying fit. This is also the starting point for the annual Barranquilla Marathon (typically held in March).

7. Cycling

Cycling enthusiasts can explore Barranquilla’s streets and neighborhoods on two wheels. With a growing emphasis on bike lanes and cycling infrastructure, the city is becoming increasingly bike-friendly. The Gran Malecón is a popular spot for cycling along with Via al Mar and nearby towns. BiciPeople and BiciLight offer group cycling events. If you’re looking to purchase a bike, check out Ciclo Special on 51b and Bike House on 84 are good options.

8. Padel

Padel tennis has taken the sports world by storm with its unique blend of athleticism, strategy, and excitement. With its distinctive court design, accessible rules, and engaging gameplay, padel tennis offers a thrilling experience for both newcomers and seasoned athletes. Barranquilla has two padel court locations, Casa Padel in Villa Campestre and Gran Padel in Le Meridiem Golf.

9. Baseball

Barranquilla is home to the best baseball team in Colombia having won 12 domestic championships and one international championship, the Serie del Caribe 2022. The new season starts in early November at the Edgar Rentería Stadium. In the meantime, if you love baseball and need to practice your swing, head over to Batting Zone with some friends.

10. Volleyball

Feel the exhilaration of competition as you engage in a spirited game of volleyball. If you’re looking to join a volleyball league, check out WeekendVoley or Mintonette for more information on joining a league. If you prefer the outdoors, the city’s numerous beaches, such as Playa Sabanilla-Country and Playa Mendoza, provide the perfect setting for beach volleyball. Gather your friends, set up a net, and spike your way to victory while enjoying the picturesque coastal views.

Conclusion

Barranquilla’s energetic spirit is epitomized by its diverse sports offerings. Whether you’re an adrenaline junkie or someone who prefers a more tranquil exercise routine, the city provides an array of choices to suit every taste. So, lace up your sneakers, grab your gear, and embark on an active journey through the lively streets and scenic landscapes of Barranquilla. The adventure awaits – are you ready to embrace the thrill?
